About Us

Built on the trail,
not in an office.

Adventure with Killah is a registered social business based in Blantyre, dedicated to transforming lives through immersive outdoor experiences. We specialise in hiking, camping, team building and personal development programmes that work the spirit, mind and body, while promoting mental wellness and environmental conservation.

Malawi is blessed with some of the most breathtaking landscapes in Africa — from majestic mountains and rolling hills to pristine lakes, forests and waterfalls. We exist to connect people with that nature, encouraging them to step outside their comfort zone and create unforgettable memories along the way.

Mission

To provide safe, exciting and transformative outdoor adventure experiences that promote teamwork, personal growth, wellness, mental health and environmental conservation.

Vision

To become the leading adventure and outdoor team-building company in Malawi and the region — inspiring people, one trail at a time.
